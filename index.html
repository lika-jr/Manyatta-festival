Manyatta Festival

Buy Samburu, Build Samburu

The Manyatta festival "Buy Samburu, Build Samburu," is a vibrant celebration of the local culture, entrepreneurship, and community pride. The festival has seen growing popularity since its launch in 2024 and has become one of Samburu's most dynamic annual events.
